The utility bills to keep it running

Sprint 1 only, at ~300 members

The mechanic analogy. You pay a mechanic for their time and skill — that is the build fee. The parts they fit (oil, filters, brake pads) are a separate cost, charged at cost, direct from the supplier. You would pay them regardless of which mechanic you used. Same here. Altitude AI is the mechanic. Hosting, AI usage, and subscriptions are the parts — paid by you directly to the providers, no markup.
