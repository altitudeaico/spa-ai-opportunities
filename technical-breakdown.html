Before anything else
Two completely separate costs — important to understand from day one
Every project has two types of cost. Getting clear on the difference upfront removes all confusion about money.
Build fee — paid to Altitude AI, once
What you pay Altitude AI for our time, expertise, and delivery. Covers designing the system, building it, testing it, and handing it to you working and documented.
Paid once per sprint. Does not come back.
Our own tools — the software we use to build — are our cost. We do not pass those through to you.
Infrastructure costs — paid by you, monthly
Every piece of software needs somewhere to live and services to run on. These go directly to the companies providing them — not through us. No markup.
Think of them as utility bills. You own the system. You pay the utilities to keep it running.
Fully transparent — we will show you exactly what each one costs before we write a single line of code.
The simplest way to think about it. You hire a builder to fit a new kitchen. You pay the builder for their time and skill — that is the build fee. But the kitchen also needs gas, electricity, and water. Those are your ongoing utility bills — paid directly to the suppliers, not through the builder. You would pay them regardless of which builder you used. That is exactly this. Altitude AI builds the system. Hosting, AI usage, and platform subscriptions are your utility bills — paid directly to the providers, fully transparent, no markup from us.

⚠ How WhatsApp group access actually works — being precise

WhatsApp does not have a button that lets a system automatically approve pending group join requests. What we build is this: the moment payment is confirmed, the system automatically sends the new member a direct group invite link via WhatsApp. They tap it and they are in. Same outcome — instant access on payment — through a method that is supported and reliable. The member experience is seamless.

What we build
The knowledge base — the AI's brain

We take everything Deb has ever created — podcast transcripts, course material, past group messages, strategy documents, watch list commentary — and load it into a system the AI can instantly search when a member asks a question.

When someone asks "should I be buying this stock right now?" the AI searches everything Deb has ever said about that kind of situation and writes a response that reflects Deb's thinking. Not a generic chatbot — specifically trained on Deb's knowledge.

Plain English: think of it as giving the AI a searchable library of everything Deb knows. When a question comes in, the AI finds the most relevant parts of that library and uses them to write the answer.

Voice and style training

Beyond the content, we train the AI to communicate the way Deb communicates. His directness, his level of detail, how he handles uncertainty, what he will and will not advise on. Responses need to sound like Deb wrote them.

We also build strict guardrails. The AI will not give specific personal investment advice. Anything that crosses into regulated financial advice is redirected to Deb personally — with a full summary of what the member asked so he has context before he replies.

WhatsApp connection

A dedicated WhatsApp number is set up through an official WhatsApp Business provider. Deb announces it to the community. Members message it, the AI responds within seconds.

Note: setting this up requires applying to Meta for business API access. This takes approximately 1-2 weeks. We apply on day one of the sprint so it does not delay delivery.

Escalation to Deb

When the AI detects a question it should not answer — personal financial situations, major investment decisions — it sends Deb a notification with the full conversation and a suggested response. Deb replies and it goes straight back to the member.

Usage monitoring

A simple dashboard showing how many questions members are asking, which topics come up most, and what the AI is costing per month. Gives Deb visibility on what his members want to know and whether the AI is handling it well.

What we build
Payment to access — automated

When someone pays on WOP, that platform sends an instant signal to our system with the new member's details. Our system immediately sends them a WhatsApp message containing a direct group invite link. They tap it, they are in. No waiting, no manual approval.

Plain English for the technical part: WOP has a feature that fires a notification automatically when a payment happens. We catch that notification and trigger the WhatsApp message. The whole thing takes about 10-15 seconds from payment to group access.

⚠ One technical limitation to be upfront about

Telegram bots can post in groups and read when someone replies or reacts. However a bot cannot send a private DM to a group member who has never messaged the bot directly first — Telegram does not allow it. So the conversion message goes as a reply in the group thread, tagging the person directly. It is slightly more public than a private DM but it works within Telegram's rules and it still drives conversion. If we want private DMs as well, we include a pinned message at the start of the revival asking people to message the bot directly to get exclusive updates — those who do can then receive private DMs.

What we build
The Telegram posting agent

A bot deployed into the existing group that posts on a schedule. Content generated automatically from the watch list and recent market commentary — written in Deb's tone with a link to the paid group in every post.

Posting schedule: daily for the first two weeks to re-establish presence, then 3-4 times per week ongoing.

Engagement detection and conversion reply

The bot monitors for reactions and replies to its posts. Anyone who engages gets a reply that references what they reacted to and presents the paid group as the natural next step — relevant rather than a generic pitch.

Content generation pipeline

Each morning the system pulls the latest watch list movements and generates a Telegram post automatically. Short, punchy, genuinely useful — content that gives people a reason to stay engaged and a reason to want more.

Conversion tracking

Every link the bot posts includes a tracking code so we know exactly how many people clicked through to WOP and how many became paying members. Precise, real-time ROI on the Telegram revival.
