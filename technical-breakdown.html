Start with WhatsApp. It is where members already are, it removes all friction, and it can be live within 30 days. The web app version becomes Sprint 4 (the watch list app) where we build both together as a unified member platform. The AI coach brain is built once — it just gets a second front door later.

What we build
🧠 Knowledge base

Every piece of content Deb has ever created is ingested, cleaned, chunked, and stored in a vector database. Podcast transcripts, course material, group message history, strategy documents, watch list logic. This becomes the brain.

When a member asks a question, the system searches this knowledge base for the most relevant context, then sends it to the AI model to generate a response in Deb's voice.

🎬 Voice and persona training

We fine-tune the AI's response style on Deb's actual communication patterns — how he phrases things, his directness, his tone on financial topics, what he would and would not say. This is not generic AI. It reads like Deb.

We also define strict guardrails — what the AI will not advise on, when it escalates to Deb personally, and how it handles questions outside the Spartan strategy.

📱 WhatsApp integration

A dedicated WhatsApp Business number connects via WABA API. Incoming member messages trigger the AI pipeline. Responses are delivered within seconds. The number appears in the group as a contact members can message directly.

📄 Escalation logic

The AI detects when a question requires personal intervention — specific financial advice, portfolio decisions, personal circumstances — and routes those to Deb with a summary of the conversation. He only sees what genuinely needs him.

📈 Usage monitoring

A simple dashboard showing message volume, most common question categories, escalation rate, and token consumption. Deb can see what his members are asking most and whether the AI is handling it well.

What we build
🔗 WOP to WhatsApp webhook

WOP has a webhook system that fires an event payload the moment a subscription payment succeeds. We catch that payload, extract the member's phone number and tier, and trigger the WhatsApp group approval — all within 10-15 seconds of payment.

This requires WOP API access (which Deb already has as the account owner) and a simple serverless function we write and host that bridges the two platforms.

💌 Tiered welcome sequence

Different welcome messages for different tiers. A £99 standard member gets a warm welcome and the essentials. A £300 Spartan gets a more personal, exclusive-feeling message that reflects their premium status and unlocks different content.

All messages are written in Deb's voice during the sprint. He reviews and approves each one before go-live.

🕐 7-day onboarding journey

A pre-written sequence of daily messages that fire automatically based on join date — not a fixed calendar. Day 1 is always their first day regardless of when they joined.

Day 1 — Welcome, what SPA is, what to do first
Day 2 — The Spartan strategy explained simply
Day 3 — How to read the watch list
Day 4 — Your first trade walkthrough
Day 5 — How to use the discussion room
Day 6 — Ask the AI coach anything
Day 7 — Check in and next steps

🚫 Offboarding automation

The mirror image of onboarding. When a subscription cancels or a payment fails, the system automatically sends a retention offer first, then removes WhatsApp access, revokes WOP permissions, and logs the departure. Nothing leaks through.

Failed payments get an automatic retry notification and a 48-hour grace period before access is removed — recovering a meaningful percentage of accidental churns.

What we build
🤖 Telegram AI posting agent

A bot deployed into the existing Telegram group that posts on a schedule Deb controls. Content is generated automatically from the watch list and market commentary — repurposed, not recycled verbatim. Posts in Deb's tone. Includes his WOP sign-up link in each post.

Posting cadence: daily for the first two weeks to re-establish presence, then 3-4 times per week ongoing. Volume can be adjusted anytime.

📩 Engagement detection and DM flow

The bot monitors group reactions and replies. Any engagement — a reaction, a reply, a message — triggers an automated DM to that person. The DM is personalised, references what they engaged with, and presents the paid WhatsApp group offer naturally rather than as a cold pitch.

DM copy is written during the sprint in Deb's voice and tested before go-live. Multiple variants to avoid repetition.

📊 Content generation pipeline

Each day, an automated pipeline pulls the latest watch list data and market movements and generates a Telegram-formatted post. Short, punchy, actionable — the kind of content that drove engagement in the group when it was active. Includes a clear call to action pointing at the paid group.

📈 Conversion tracking dashboard

A simple tracking layer that shows how many people the bot DM'd, how many clicked the WOP link, and how many converted to paying members. This tells us the ROI of the Telegram group precisely and in real time.

Revenue potential
📈 Conservative conversion modelling

4,000 contacts. Conservative 1% conversion to paid membership at £99/month = £3,960 new MRR. That is the entire build fee recovered in the first month from this sprint alone. At 2% conversion it is £7,920 MRR. This is the fastest return on investment of any sprint in the roadmap.

Risks and mitigations
⚠
Telegram spam detection. Sending automated DMs to large numbers of people can trigger Telegram anti-spam limits. We pace the DM sending carefully — no more than 30-50 per day initially — and scale up gradually once the bot establishes a clean history.

Group quality. Some of those 4,000 contacts may be inactive accounts or spam. The bot's engagement detection naturally filters for real humans — only people who actually respond get entered into the conversion funnel.

What we build (Option A)
📱 Member-facing app

Clean, mobile-first web app. Members log in via their WOP account or a separate auth. They see the watch list, live prices, Deb's signal status on each stock, and a personalised news feed for the stocks they hold.

⚙ Admin interface

A private admin view for Deb or a VA. Add and remove stocks. Set signal status. Add notes or commentary for each stock. Changes appear to all members instantly.

🔔 Push alert system

When Deb changes a stock status to Buy or Sell in the admin, a WhatsApp notification fires automatically to all members in the relevant tier. Plain English explanation attached (from Sprint 1 AI).

📰 Personalised news feed

Each member sees news articles relevant to the stocks on the watch list, filtered by what they have marked as personally held. Pulled from a financial news API, summarised by AI into plain English. Updated throughout the trading day.

👥 Member authentication

Secure login tied to WOP subscription status. If a member cancels, access is revoked automatically (connecting to Sprint 2). New members get access automatically on payment. No separate sign-up process.

What we build
🤖 Avatar and voice creation

Using HeyGen, Deb records 30-60 minutes of natural footage and 10-15 minutes of clean audio. HeyGen trains a personalised avatar model and ElevenLabs clones the voice. The result is a digital twin that can deliver any script with Deb's face, expressions, and voice.

This is a one-time setup. Once trained, the avatar can produce unlimited videos. The quality improves with more training footage — we recommend an initial session and a top-up session after 30 days.

📝 AI script generation pipeline

A pipeline that generates video scripts automatically from source material. Three content pillars:

Watch list commentary — today's key stock, what to watch, the signal
Spartan strategy explainers — educational 60-second breakdowns
Member win highlights — social proof in video format

Scripts are 30, 60, or 90 seconds — optimised for the platform they are destined for. Deb or a VA reviews a weekly batch and approves before rendering.

🎬 Programmatic video rendering

Approved scripts are fed into HeyGen via API — not manually. The pipeline renders videos automatically, overnight, and outputs finished files ready for editing. A week of content can be produced in a single automated run.

✏ AI-assisted editing

Each rendered video is processed through an editing pipeline — auto-captions generated by Whisper, brand overlays added, background music applied, platform-specific crops created (9:16 for Reels/TikTok, 16:9 for YouTube). Output is platform-ready without human editing.

📅 Scheduling and distribution

Finished videos are uploaded automatically to a scheduling tool (Buffer or Publer). Posting times are set to peak engagement windows for each platform. A week of content posts automatically from one Monday approval session.

The human audit layer
👁 Deb's concern — addressed directly

On the call Deb specifically said he does not want AI content to be so robotic that people can tell. He wants a human audit touch. Here is how we build that in: the review step in the pipeline is non-optional. Scripts go to Deb or a VA for approval before any video is rendered. No video is published without a human seeing it first. The AI does the heavy lifting. The human catches anything that does not sound right. This is the same model Deb already uses for his newsletters — his ghostwriter uses AI, he approves. We just apply the same model to video.
